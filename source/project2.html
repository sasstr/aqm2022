We were approached by a long-term contact with an intriguing request.

Our brief: to design an innovative and modern childcare centre in Monaco.

Bringing a new concept in childcare to Monaco, our goal was to create a colourful, flexible space where children could actively construct their own learning.

Key to this was the creation of several distinct spaces, so children across a range of ages and stages could explore and develop their areas of interest. With specialty studios covering construction, science, arts, crafts and music, through to a main play area incorporating a state of the art indoor

playground specially designed with children’s safety and learning in mind, and a boutique play area for smaller groups of children, we endeavoured to create an area for everyone to play and learn at their own pace. Set to open in 2021, we cannot wait to see this vision be brought to life.
